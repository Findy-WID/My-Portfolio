About Me

I am a Smart Contract Developer with strong hands-on experience designing, implementing, testing, and integrating secure, modular Solidity contracts using Hardhat, Foundry, and Viem. I specialize in building event-driven contracts and connecting them to production-ready Next.js/React frontends.

While not yet deployed on mainnet, I’ve built multiple real-world contracts end-to-end (ERC20, reward systems, voting, savings contracts) and collaborated in multi-developer environments. I am deeply committed to growing into a world-class blockchain engineer and currently building SliqPay, a Web2/Web3 fintech platform for African markets.

I approach every project with purpose, excellence, and a heart to make a lasting impact. Let's create something meaningful together.
